About

VHetu is a leading service based Information technology, consulting and outsourcing services provider. VHetuis provides small and mid-sized businesses, helping them with innovative technology solutions to disrupt existing businesses and creating new opportunities.

VHetu Startup division partners with entrepreneurs to help build and grow their startups. While It make all kinds of web and mobile applications, this division specializes in getting Minimum Viable Product (MVP) online for feedback and initial sales. We take the idea, no matter how grandiose or petite and chisel it into an ideal MVP and release to the world quickly.

VHetu industry experience spans manufacturing, hospitality, health care, government, media, ISVs and financial services. With presence in the US and India, the company delivers comprehensive services for mission critical operational systems, search and recommendation systems, business intelligence and analytics, cloud enablement and mobile enablement.
